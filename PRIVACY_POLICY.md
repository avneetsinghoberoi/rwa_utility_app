# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 2026

## 1. Introduction

GateBasic ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our application.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, residential address, flat/house number
- **Payment Information:** Transaction details, payment methods (processed through secure gateways)
- **Profile Information:** Profile photo, personal preferences, contact details
- **Communications:** Messages, complaints, issue descriptions, feedback

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Features accessed, actions performed, time spent on features
- **Location Data:** General location (city, state) from address provided
- **Crash Reports:** Application performance and error logs

### 2.3 Firebase Services

We use Firebase (by Google) for:
- **Authentication:** Secure login and account management
- **Firestore Database:** Storing user data and application content
- **Cloud Storage:** Storing documents and images
- **Cloud Messaging:** Sending push notifications
- **Analytics:** Understanding app usage patterns
- **Crashlytics:** Monitoring application crashes

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

- **Service Delivery:** Creating and managing your account, processing payments, delivering notices
- **Communication:** Sending updates, notifications, support messages
- **Security:** Detecting fraud, maintaining security, preventing abuse
- **Analytics:** Understanding user behavior, improving features
- **Legal Compliance:** Meeting regulatory requirements
- **Customer Support:** Responding to inquiries and resolving issues

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Within the Application

Your information is visible to:
- **Society Administrators:** For account management and payment processing
- **Other Residents:** Limited profile information (name, house number, phone, email) in the Members Directory
- **Payment Gateways:** Payment information for transaction processing

### 4.2 Third-Party Services

We share data with:
- **Firebase/Google:** For authentication, storage, and analytics
- **Payment Processors:** For payment verification
- **Cloud Providers:** For data storage and backup

### 4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when:
- Required by law or court orders
- Necessary to protect our legal rights
- Required for fraud prevention or security

---

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Security Measures

- **Firebase Authentication:** Secure login with password hashing
- **Firestore Security Rules:** Role-based access control
- **Encryption:** Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- **Access Control:** Only authorized administrators can access sensitive data

### 5.2 Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while account is active, deleted upon account deletion
- **Payment Records:** Retained for 7 years for legal/tax compliance
- **Deleted Data:** Permanently removed from our systems
