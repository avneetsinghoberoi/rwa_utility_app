Features

Everything in one place

Built to replace scattered WhatsApp groups, Excel sheets, and manual follow-ups — for good.

Smart Payments
Auto-generated monthly invoices. Residents upload proof, admins verify in one tap. No more chasing.
Complaint Tracking
Residents raise issues digitally. Admins update status and give feedback. Everything is on record.
Digital Notices
Post announcements instantly. Every resident gets notified. No missed messages, no confusion.
Expense Reports
Log monthly expenses and share them with residents. Full financial transparency builds trust.
Member Management
Owners, tenants, family — all under one account. Add or remove members with a tap.
QR Entry Pass
Every resident gets a unique QR code with name, house, and vehicle details for seamless gate access.
